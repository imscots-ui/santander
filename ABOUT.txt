What this is
------------

A self-initiated concept and working prototype exploring how Santander
Business Banking workflows could move from paper to digital — covering
mandate changes, account closures, business detail updates, bulk
payments, KYC/KYB re-verification, and HMRC Making Tax Digital.

This was built outside core working hours as a thinking exercise — a way
to demonstrate what's possible rather than a finished proposal. The aim
is to start a conversation about where Business Banking goes next, and
to contribute ideas to that conversation.


Status
------

This is a prototype only. It has no backend, no real data, no real
customer information, and no connection to live Santander or HMRC
systems. Everything shown is illustrative.

It is not a competing product, not commercial work, and not intended
for use outside internal Santander discussions about future direction.
