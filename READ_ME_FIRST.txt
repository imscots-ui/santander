SANTANDER BUSINESS BANKING — CONCEPT BUNDLE
============================================

Hello,

Thanks for taking a look. This bundle contains a self-initiated concept
piece I built outside core hours, exploring how Santander Business
Banking could move its main customer workflows from paper to digital.

Nothing in here is live — no real data, no backend, no system
connections. Every workflow, every screen, and every regulatory
reference is built out properly, but as a concept demonstration only.

WHAT THE PROTOTYPE COVERS
-------------------------

  - One app for mobile and desktop, same state throughout
  - All seven UK business entity types — compliance paths diverge per type
  - Per-account mandates including dual-signature unlock
  - Eleven paperless self-serve workflows replacing paper forms:
      Account closure, Partner unreachable escalation, Mandate changes,
      Bulk payments / wages, Business details update, Dormancy
      reactivation, MTD VAT submission, Personal/business account
      unlink, Credit ring-fence, Pre-approved lending, International FX
  - Business health score (5-factor live gauge)
  - 13-week cash flow forecast (SVG bar chart)
  - Supplier risk radar (Companies House filing status per counterparty)
  - Director command centre (KYC status, last active, pending actions)
  - Smart payment sequencer (optimise button maximises minimum balance)
  - Voice memo → MTD ledger (transcription, extract, one-tap confirm)
  - Receipt scan → MTD (OCR extraction, category, digital link)
  - Voice ID biometrics (3-phrase enrolment, cross-channel, GDPR Art.9)
  - Live notification bell (anomaly alerts, approvals, cooling-off)
  - Open banking PSD2 consent audit (scope, expiry, one-tap revoke)
  - Statements with counterparty search and payment-method filters
  - Built-in HMRC Making Tax Digital module (full VAT100 submission)
  - Continuous KYC / KYB with GOV.UK One Login framing
  - Consumer Duty and FCA SYSC 10A throughout
  - 7-year audit trail per FCA SYSC 9


WHY I BUILT THIS
----------------

I work at Santander as a Business Banking Advisor and process the
paper forms every day. The customer pain points are real, the volume
is high, and the competitive pressure from Tide and Starling on the
SMB segment is growing. I started thinking about what good looks
like and built something to test the idea.

The aim is to start a conversation — not to claim ownership of the
direction or to propose anything specific about my role. If any of
the thinking is useful, I'd be glad to discuss it.
